Theme: Success through Passion and Purpose

Stage 11 - Writing a formal blog post

🚀 Project Mission

Write a formal blog post about a successful person — this could be an athlete, leader, influencer, or artist — who has made a strong impact in their field or community. Let your writing reflect both admiration and analysis!

✏️ Your Mission

Step 1

Choose one successful person who inspires you.

Step 2

Research their background, qualities, challenges, and achievements.

Step 3

Organize your ideas in a digital mind map (Canva, Prezi, or Google Slides).

Step 4

Write a formal blog post (180–250 words) that:

  • Follows a clear structure (introduction, body, conclusion)
  • Uses a formal tone and transition phrases

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the goal of the project?

Can I write about anyone?
