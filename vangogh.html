Vincent van Gogh – Passion and Pain on Canvas

About Vincent van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh (1853–1890) was a Dutch painter whose emotionally expressive work and bold use of color helped to shape modern art. Despite personal struggles, his legacy continues to inspire creativity and resilience.

Key Achievements

  • Created more than 2,000 artworks including Starry Night and Sunflowers.
  • Influenced the Post-Impressionist movement and generations of artists.
  • Demonstrated perseverance and passion despite hardship and mental health challenges.

Inspiring Quote

"I dream my painting and I paint my dream."
